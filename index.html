Tanks

Tanks is a 2D top down arcade game designed mainly to test and identify useful features for SimpleEngine.

Pitch

You are a green tank fighting endless waves of red tanks until you die. You get one point for each enemy destroyed. You loose one health point when an enemy hits you. When your hps are at 0, the game is over.
